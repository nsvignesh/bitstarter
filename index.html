What is Market Nav?

Market Nav is an android app that helps you effortlessly navigate through your preferred supermarkets ensuring a quicker and cheaper shop ping experience. Market Nav lets you enter and save your favourite shopping list and gives you the best route from the time you enter your market to the time you are ready for billing. It also presents you with a host of supermarket maps. So are you ready to make your shopping experiences faster and much more enjoyable??? Gear up! Market Nav is here!!!!

The Rollout Plan

Market Nav is planned in collaboration with one of the biggest and popular super-market chains "Shop and Stop". The project requires investment for mapping supermarkets which is to be funded by Shop and Stop. It also requires location sensing sensors to detect user presence and notify your android phones and also to identify item locations. The software in itself will be built using the Android OS alng with your phones Bluetooth and Wireless hardware features. Thus as mentioned above, the project is partly funded by the "Shop and Stop" chain while the rest is to be crowdfunded.
